The End Of The Innocence

Written by Bruce Hornsby and Don Henley
Performed regularly by Bob Dylan during the fall 2002 tour
Tabbed by Eyolf Østrem

The intro is playable in G, but the two highest strings that seem to be sounding throughout made me think they might be open strings, hence capo 3rd fret and key of E. I don't think that's what they're playing, but it sounds better that way, so I added a tab like that (see below).
I should also say that my copy of this song isn't the best-sounding ever, so there are probably mistakes.
