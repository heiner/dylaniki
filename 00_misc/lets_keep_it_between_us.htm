Let's Keep It Between Us

Written by Bob Dylan
Performed during the gospel tours in 1980
Tabbed by Eyolf Østrem

Played (with Bob at the piano) in Eb major, which isn't very nice of him.
Here are two capoed alternatives, with capo on 1st and 3rd fret respectively.

The most distinctive chord here is the one I've called F6; it's a huge chord, with something of F, something of Dm, quite a lot of D7-10 (x5456x) in it, but F6 is a fair approximation.
The same would have to be said about some of the other “grand” chords (at “trust” and the last “keep”) - they are approximations. You can't have it all on one single guitar (then again it's amazing how much you can have)
