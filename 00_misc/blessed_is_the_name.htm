Blessed is the Name

Written by Bob Dylan
Performed during the 1979-80 gospel tours.
Tabbed by Eyolf Østrem (but see alternate lyric transcriptions below)

Ironically, this is probably the song with which Dylan has made the most energetic attempts at a sing-along kind of rapport with the audience, but – it doesn't really work, does it...?
The lyrics for the verses – well, I made an attempt. The transcriptions below are a mixture of phonetic and semantic. I welcome corrections, as always.
