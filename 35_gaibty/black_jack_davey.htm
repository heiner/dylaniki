Black Jack Davey

Trad. /arr. Bob Dylan
Released on Good As I Been To You (1992) and played live occasionally in 1993
Tabbed by Eyolf Østrem

Let the 2nd string sound during the Gm strumming – that's what creates that delightful clash between the Bb of the melody and the B of the strumming.

And, of course, there are plenty of variations from one verse to another.

Capo 4th fret (sounding key B minor)
