In My Time of Dyin'

Sung by Bob Dylan on the self-titled album (1961)
Tabbed by Eyolf Østrem

The song is basically just a finger-picking/bottleneck exercise over a sustained chord in Open D/E tuning. It's all in the singing... :-)

Open D tuning (D-A-d-f#-a-d')/Capo 4th fret or (preferably, since the 12th fret is easier to hit then) Open E tuning (E-B-e-g#-b-e')/capo 2nd fret.

Below is the intro and the first verse tabbed the way it is played. All the slide figures are played with the bottleneck, but if you haven't emptied your bottle yet, the fingers will have to do.

The lick that is played here and there in the intro and between lines eventually settles into a more fixed form:
