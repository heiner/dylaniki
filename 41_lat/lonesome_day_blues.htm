There are some interesting references to Mark Twain's Huckleberry Finn here (thanks to Nick for the informing me):

*) midway through chapter 17: '...and my sister Mary Ann run off and got married and never was heard of no more...'

**) Towards the end of Ch. 1: 'I felt so lonesome I most wished I was dead. The stars was shining, and the leaves rustled in the woods ever so mournful;...and the wind was trying to whisper something to me and I couldn't make out what it was.' (thanks to Nick for the reference)
