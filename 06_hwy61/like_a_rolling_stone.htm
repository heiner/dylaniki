Like a Rolling Stone

Words and music Bob Dylan
Released on Highway 61 Revisited (1965), Greatest Hits (1967) and Biograph (1985), in an alternate version on The Bootleg Series 1-3 (1991) and in live versions on Self Portrait (1970), Before the Flood (1974), At Budokan (1978), Unplugged (1995), Live 1966 (1998), and No direction home (2005)
Tabbed by Eyolf Østrem

Note the similarity with She's Your Lover Now.
