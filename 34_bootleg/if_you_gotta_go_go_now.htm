Words and music Bob Dylan

Bootleg Series 1-3 version

The phrase end lick is basically a succession of Gs and Bbs, with some prominence to the Bb.

The G - C/g - G figure is somewhat more elaborate here than elsewhere. The el-guitar playes a little figure that would have to be played something like:

  G       C/g   G
  |   .   .   .     |   .   .

|-----------------|----------
|-----------------|----------
